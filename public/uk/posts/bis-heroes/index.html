  • Barbarian|Chieftain
    • Slot 1: Lover’s Quarrel 🠆 Moltin’ Crabcracker

    • Needs EVA build:

  • Trickster
    • Needs EVA build:
      • Slot 1: Primarosa 🠆 Somnus Boltcaster
    • Does not need EVA build:
      • Slot 1: Primarosa 🠆 Somnus Boltcaster

    • Slot 5: Slide Grease 🠆 Sandman’s Pouch

    • Note: If your hero rolled Marksman use Primarosa 🠆 Somnus Boltcaster

    • Note: Use 2x Slide Grease for more survivability, or 2x Sandman’s Pouch for more ATK
    • Slot 4: Pot of Paella 🠆 Sandman’s Pouch
