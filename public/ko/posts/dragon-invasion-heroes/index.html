Hero builds for Dragon Invasion, and not much else

The hero builds on this page are expensive, and very unbalanced! They will perform very poorly for normal questing.

They are exclusively for Dragon Invasion or leaderboard heroes - if you want balanced heroes for clearing quests refer to the tl;dr page.

Please seek help on the STC Discord or the Official Discord before considering these builds. Or don’t, ’tis your time/gems!
